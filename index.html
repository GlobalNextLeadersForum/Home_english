What is GNLF?

Global Next Leaders Forum (GNLF) is the international circle organized by students from the University of Tokyo and others, established in 2010.The 14th members participated in this year. Once a year, the forum invites students and professors from about 10 countries and regions. We aim to nurture the next generation of global leaders through this forum.

About Forum

The annual Forum is a 10-day program that includes discussions, presentations, and fieldwork, providing students with a deep understanding of social issues and a chance to learn. In addition, students will have the opportunity to spend time with students from other countries, which will provide them with new values and stimulation.

How To Apply

Applicants to GNLF2022 must submit an entry form. Applicants must be undergraduate or graduate students as of October 22, 2022 to be eligible to apply. Selection will be made based on the entry sheet, and those who pass the selection process will be officially qualified to participate.
